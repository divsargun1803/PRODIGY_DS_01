# PRODIGY_DS_01 — World Population Distribution

**Prodigy InfoTech | Data Science Internship | Task 01**

## Objective
Create a bar chart or histogram to visualize the distribution of a categorical or continuous variable — here, world population distribution by country, region, and income group using World Bank 2022 data.

## Visualizations
| Chart | Type | Variable |
|---|---|---|
| Top 15 Most Populous Countries | Horizontal Bar | Continuous (population) |
| Population by Region | Bar Chart | Categorical (region) |
| Population by Income Group | Bar Chart | Categorical (income group) |
| Country Population Distribution | Histogram | Continuous (population in millions) |

## Key Insights
- Population distribution is **strongly right-skewed** — most countries have under 200M people, but China and India alone exceed 1 billion each
- **South Asia + East Asia** account for nearly half the world's population
- **Lower-middle income countries** hold the largest share of global population, highlighting the scale of the development challenge
- The **mean (~159M) far exceeds the median (~52M)**, driven by a handful of extreme outliers

## Dataset
World Bank Open Data — [SP.POP.TOTL indicator](https://data.worldbank.org/indicator/SP.POP.TOTL) | 50 countries | 2022 estimates
